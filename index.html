We have made the difficult decision to postpone the
ResBaz Qld Conference due to the COVID and flu peak forecast for Brisbane.

We've reached this decision after much consideration, as the health and safety of our attendees, teachers, helpers and volunteers are our top priority.

We aim to reschedule Resbaz Qld to the 1-3rd Nov. All attendees will be refunded.

We would like to thank everyone who has been working with us so far, and hope to continue working with you to bring Resbaz together later in the year.
